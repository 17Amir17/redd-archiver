Boolean Logic:

  • "quoted phrases" for exact matches
  • word1 word2 searches for both words (implicit AND)
  • word1 OR word2 to match either term (uppercase OR required)
  • -exclude to remove terms (minus prefix)

Filters:

  • sub:technology or subreddit:technology to filter by subreddit
  • author:username or user:username to filter by author
  • score:10+ to filter by minimum score
  • type:post or type:comment to filter content type

Sort Options:

  • sort:relevance or sort:rank - by search relevance (default)
  • sort:score - by upvotes (highest first)
  • sort:new, sort:newest, or sort:date - newest first
  • sort:old or sort:oldest - oldest first

Example: "open source" OR privacy sub:technology score:10+ sort:score
